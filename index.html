Beardon

A wizard is causing trouble with the local bears and made a portal that has sucked you in.

You are now stuck on an unknown world.

You make a deal with the bears to help stop the wizard in exchange they help you get home.

Zeff

Zeff used to be a normal blue bird experimenting with magic.

On his travels to another world, he got obsessed with power.

The darkness consumed him.

Through doing quest you will learn the wizards weaknesses and gain trust with the bears.

With the knowledge received from the quests you get the choice of how you deal with the wizard Zeff.
